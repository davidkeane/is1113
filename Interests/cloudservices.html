About Us

Cloud Lab is a company that deals with selling cloud computing products, based in Cork, Ireland. It was set up
in 2018 by a group of students from University College Cork.
Cloud Lab is a leading expert in cloud computing, employing highly skilled and knowledgable staff at its Cork headquarters.
Cloud Lab's aim is to make the purchase of cloud computing quick and easy for its customers.

About Our Products

Gmail

Gmail is a free, advertising-supported email service developed by Google. Users can access Gmail on the web and using third-party programs.
Gmail offers users 15 gigabytes of storage and allows users to send emails up to 25 megabytes in size. Emails of a greater capacity can be sent
through the use of Google drive. Gmail has 1.2 billion users worldwide. Click here to visit Gmail website.
